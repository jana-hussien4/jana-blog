Hi, I'm Jana, a Dev Degree intern at Shopify.

Dev Degree is a work-integrated learning program that combines a full computer science degree with four years of hands-on software engineering at Shopify. You're not an intern in the traditional sense. You're a developer with real responsibilities, working on real codebases, shipping real code, while earning your degree at the same time. It's intense in the best way.

This blog is my public learning journal. Every issue I work through teaches me something: about HTTP, distributed systems, Ruby, Rails, or just how to read a stack trace without panicking. I write it down here so I don't forget it, and so maybe you don't have to hit the same walls I did.

Most of my learning starts with something breaking.
